About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Engineering at the University of Toronto, advised by Prof. Andreas Moshovos. My research focuses on building resource-efficient AI systems, including AI data compression and bit-level optimization, efficient neural network training, efficient processing for 3D animatable avatars, and systems for neuromorphic computing.

Previously, I received an M.Sc. (GPA 3.9) and B.Sc. (GPA 4.0, rank 2/260) in Electronics and Communications from Cairo University.
